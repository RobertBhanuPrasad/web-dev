Frequently Asked Questions

HDPE pipes offer superior corrosion resistance, flexibility, and a 50+ year service life. Unlike metal pipes, they won't rust or scale, and unlike PVC, they handle high pressures and temperatures without becoming brittle.

We offer pipes in pressure ratings from PN 2.5 to PN 16, covering SDR 33 down to SDR 11. This range supports water supply, irrigation, gas distribution, and industrial fluid transport applications.

HDPE pipes can be joined using butt fusion, electrofusion fittings, or mechanical couplings. Butt fusion creates a monolithic, leak-proof joint stronger than the pipe itself, making it ideal for buried and underwater installations.

Our pipes are used across water supply and distribution, agricultural irrigation, mining slurry transport, sewage systems, fishnet manufacturing, industrial process piping, and underground cable ducting.

Mangalam HDPE Pipes are BIS certified (IS 4984), ISO 4427 compliant, and CE marked. Every batch undergoes hydrostatic pressure testing, wall thickness measurement, and material density verification before dispatch.

Advanced HDPE Pipe Manufacturing Process

Our state-of-the-art extrusion technology ensures consistent quality, optimal material properties, and dimensional accuracy in every pipe we manufacture.

High-Grade Raw Material Selection

Vacuum sizing tanks ensure precise outer diameter while internal pressure maintains perfect roundness and wall thickness uniformity.

  • PE100 grade material
  • Optimal molecular weight distribution

Precision Extrusion Molding

Advanced twin-screw extruders with precise temperature control zones ensure consistent melt homogeneity and optimal processing conditions for superior pipe quality.

  • Temperature-controlled extrusion zones
  • Consistent melt flow index

Controlled Water Cooling Bath

Precision-temperature water baths rapidly cool the extruded pipe, locking in dimensional accuracy and structural integrity throughout the wall thickness.

  • Multi-stage cooling tanks
  • Precise temperature regulation

Precision Sizing & Calibration

Vacuum sizing sleeves maintain exact outer diameter tolerance ensuring every pipe meets dimensional specifications to within ±0.1mm.

  • ±0.1mm diameter tolerance
  • Vacuum-assisted calibration

Rigorous Quality Control Testing

Each pipe undergoes comprehensive testing for wall thickness, roundness, pressure resistance, and material integrity before leaving our facility.

  • Hydrostatic pressure testing

Laser Printing & Product Marking

High-resolution laser printing applies product identification, specifications, and traceability codes permanently onto every pipe surface.

  • Full batch traceability
